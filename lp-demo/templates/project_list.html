Dial Maker

I made this for my dad. He restores old radios and needs to replace the dials that appear on the radio. This makes it easy to create images of dial markers to play with. This first version is written in processing but I plan to redo it with javascript and canvas. He has a nice list of features to add to this. :-)

Painted Minis

In 2011 I started playing Dungeons and Dragons with a group of friends and after a while we decided we wanted to paint miniatures for the characters. I liked it so much I decided to keep going, painting some of their minis and painting minis for many of the monsters we face.
